Extraction guidance

One reusable professional idea per JSON object: a principle, process, risk, formula, checklist item, or decision rule. Split compound sections into multiple objects instead of one long summary.

Good: “NEC compensation events require written notice within 8 weeks of awareness — late notice risks entitlement.”
Weak: “Communication is important on NEC projects.” (filler, not actionable)

Page references, short quotes, and section notes let you verify and defend knowledge during review, consulting, and course design. Never invent page numbers not present in the source text.

Generic business platitudes, restated headings without insight, motivational language, and “importance of” statements without mechanisms. Templates with avoid generic filler add rules to suppress these patterns.
